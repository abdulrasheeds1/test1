CHAPTER 2 - THREE TRIGGERS TO CREATIVITY

THE 3 FACTORS :

    • More clarity about achieving your goal then you will become more creativity.
  2. PRESSING PROBLEMS

    • Always ask questions about current situation.It can stimulates the creativity.

CHAPTER 3 - THE MINDSTORMING METHOD

It is way of using FOCUSED QUESTIONS for concentrating the power of the mind on single questions.

20 IDEA METHOD :

  1. Write the pressing problems or goal ----> how to remove the problem or achieve the goal ?.Be discipline to generate 20 questions.
    • 1-4 should be easier one ,Next 5 should be quite difficult,Next 10 shold be more difficuilt.
    • Last 20th should more difficult to answer than all previous questions .It can be the breakthrough and transformation of the life.
  2. Be discipline to generate 20 answers for the question.
  3. Review them and select atleast one idea implement it immediately.
'YOU WILL HAVE INSIGHTS AND IDEAS THAT WILL AMAZE YOU AND THE PEOPLE AROUND YOU'.

CHAPTER 4 - QUESTIONING TO STIMULATE CREATIVITY

Asking better questions = more creative thinking.

  1. WHAT ARE WE TRYING TO DO ?
    • What we want and what we are try to accomplish.
  2. HOW ARE WE TRYING TO DO IT?
    • How we accomplish the thing that we want.
  3. WHAT RESULT OR OUTCOME DO YOU DESIRE ?
    • If we accomplish what is the outcome.
  4. 4)ARE THER OTHER WAYS TO ACHIEVE OUR GOALS OR DESIRED OUTCOMES ?
    • If we were not doing it in this way, then what is the other way to accomplish it ,it can easier ,faster and better?.

Always think "THERE IS A BETTER WAY ,MORE EFFICIENT METHOD TO ACHIEVE ANY GOAL.THERE ALWAYS SUPERIOR WAY TO USE YOUR SPECIAL TALENTS AND RESOURCES YOU HAVE".
